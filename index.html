About Us

Landmark Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the underprivileged and bringing positive change to our community. Founded with a vision to make a real difference, we work tirelessly to provide essential support, education, and resources to those who need it most. Our mission is simple: to uplift lives and inspire hope. Through your generous donations, we are able to run impactful programs, deliver aid to vulnerable families, and create opportunities for a better future. Together, we believe we can build a world where every person has the chance to thrive. Join us on this journey -- your support truly matters. Thank you for standing with us.

Our Work

Education Support

We provide educational materials and scholarships for underprivileged children.

Food & Nutrition Program

We distribute food packs to families in need and run community kitchens.

Healthcare Assistance

Free medical camps and basic health support for marginalized communities.

Emergency Relief

We respond to natural disasters with timely aid and rehabilitation support.

Community Development

We run skill development and awareness programs to empower local communities.
